I am a PhD student in Political Science at the University of Gothenburg since fall 2023. My research interests include comparative politics, political economy, and coalition formation. In my dissertation, I examine how political parties cooperate during times characterized by economic difficulties.

I have a BA in economics from Linköping university, a BA in political science from Uppsala university, and a MA in political science from Stockholm university.
